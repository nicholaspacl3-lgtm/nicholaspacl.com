About Me

A little more about me

I’m a cross-cultural creative and builder. My journey moves from rural Texas dirt roads to Australian coastlines, from the music pulse of Austin to the night markets of Taiwan—each place shaping how I design, write, and live.

Explore my timelineView photos

Who I am

I’m a curious generalist who connects dots across disciplines—product, writing, and community. I believe in slow craft, bold ideas, and building with empathy. When I’m not tinkering with interfaces, you’ll find me capturing street scenes on film, cooking for friends, or learning a new phrase in the language of wherever I call home.

Values

Kindness, curiosity, and craft—building things that help people, and doing it thoughtfully.

Strengths

Systems thinking with a storyteller’s touch—turning complex into clear, and ideas into action.

Now

Exploring culture and technology in Taiwan, collaborating remotely, and growing new creative projects.
